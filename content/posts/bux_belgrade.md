## The story of establishing BUX's first Engineering Hub in Serbia and the role I played in its success

- As a seasoned multidisciplinary professional in the fintech sector, I spearheaded the establishment of BUX's first Engineering Hub in Belgrade, a strategic move that marked a new chapter in the company's growth.
- This initiative represented a significant expansion of BUX's operational footprint, and enabled the company to have an access to a wider pool of engineering talent.
- I managed all operational aspects of this project, ensuring smooth functioning from its inception to daily operations.

![BUX Serbia Office](/img/bux_belgrade_1.png)

## My Role

- *Collaboration with Experts:* I partnered with legal and financial professionals to navigate Serbia's business regulations and laws, ensuring our operations were fully compliant and optimally structured.
- *Selecting and Equipping the Office:* I led the search and setup of our office space, focusing on a location that fostered growth and innovation. This involved not only securing the space but also ensuring it was fully equipped with the necessary technology and comforts for our team.
- *Ensuring a Productive Workspace:* A top priority was to provide our team with the essential tools and a supportive work environment. This meant equipping them with high-quality tech and creating a workspace conducive to productivity and comfort.
- *Talent Acquisition:* A key part of my role was to identify and recruit top engineering talent. I focused on assembling a team capable of driving innovation in financial technology, maintaining the high standards BUX is known for. This led to the formation of a highly skilled team that significantly contributed to several key projects.

## Cultural Integration & Local PR Efforts

- I facilitated the cultural integration of the Serbian team with the broader BUX culture, ensuring a cohesive work environment that embraced the diverse backgrounds of our team members.
- I also reached out to local media and managed to get a feature in the [region's most read online tech publication, Netokracija](https://www.netokracija.rs/milan-stevanovic-bux-beograd-197096), which significantly boosted the rate at which candidates applied for our vacancies.

![BUX Serbia Team](/img/bux_belgrade_2.png)

## The Hub's Impact on BUX

The establishment of the Engineering Hub in Belgrade has been a major step for BUX, enabling us to:
- Access a wider pool of engineering talent.
- Enhance our product development capabilities.
- Strengthen our position in the European fintech market.

The Belgrade Engineering Hub has not only expanded BUX's capabilities but also stands as a testament to our innovative spirit and commitment to embracing diverse talent pools.
